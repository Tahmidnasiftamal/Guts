Birth

Guts was born from the corpse of his lynched mother underneath a hanging tree. Afterbirth he was left to die alone in blood. Eventually a group of mercenary lead by the man named “Gambino” saw him there and thought he was dead. But soon realized that he was alive. Then, one of Gambino's acquaintance named “Shisu” took him and looked after baby Guts.

Triggers

Haunted by the trauma of being abused, raped, betrayed and sold under Gambino's shelter. But, significantly the "Brand Of Sacrifice" is what haunts him the most.

Motive

He wants to save Casca from every evil being ever born. Besides, he wants to find Griffith and rest of the "Hand of God" and kill them. But before all these, he needs to earn, kill and survive the evil spirits.
